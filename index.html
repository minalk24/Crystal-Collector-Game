How to Play

You will given random number of text at the start of the game. There are four crystals below By clicking on a crystals you will add a specific amount of point to your total score. Ypu win the game by matching your total score to random number, you lose the game if your total score goes up above random number. The value of each crystal is hidden from you until ypou click on it. Each time the game starts, the game will change the values of each crystal.
